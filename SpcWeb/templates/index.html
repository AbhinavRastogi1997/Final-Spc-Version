About Placement Fest

The Placement Fest, will introduce students to the tough challenges of placement, will entertain students and certainly guide students to a better future. The placement fest will be conducted such that it has an ever-lasting positive influence on the whole DTU Campus.

Join us for week long Placement Fest, a journey through all the events, and best practices that will keep you looking ahead.

Attend sessions and interact with other students and experts. Whether you're with us in DTU or tuning in from around the country, there's event for everyone. This year's event will be a benchmark with a lot of events, interactive experiences and the opportunity to meet one-on-one with the leaders who are shaping the future of this country.

About SPC

The first of its kind Council in Delhi Technological University where students find a platform to test their aptitude for the corporate world by making better use of the placement opportunities.


Mission

To establish an environment of professionalism which shall serve the university and the nation in the world educational community.

Events Planned

Know about the Events. SPC DTU will conduct following events during and before the placement fest. Every event is carefully planned to give students exactly the right knowledge of a perticular skill, be it an entertainment event or the Lime Light Events.

Inaugeration Event
Inauguration Event Marks the start of fest!

Inauguration Ceremony by a very renowned personality. The event will host a gathering of 400+ students and mark the start of the fest.

Mock Placement Event
Mock Placements Aptitude Test, GDs and Personal Interviews

Mock Placement Events By renowned company professionals will be the highlight of the whole fest.

Star Night
Star Night Celebrity appearance, Entertainment

Star Night: The fest will finally close its doors with a star night where popular celebrity will be called for entertainment event like concert or dance performance!

Bollywood Dance Competition
Bollywood Dance Competition Bollywood, Entertainment

This will also be the highlight of the fest since renowned professionals from bollywood would judge the dance competition and thus provide one lucky winner her/ his first break in bollywood!

Fun Events
Fun Events Entertainment, Recreation

A lot of small entertainment events will keep the fest going and create the atmosphere of playfulness around the campus. These events will be crucial for getting crowd attraction towards the fest and its events.

Social event

FAQ

Here is a list of answers to frequently asked questions about Student Placement Council.
These questions will try to solve all possible doubts about the Placement Fest in your mind!

  • 01. General FAQ
  • 02. About SPC
Will the students be able to get actual placements?

No, The purpose of the fest is to provide corporate exposure to students.

Can I attend all the rounds of Mock Placements

Yes, you need to be a college student and have a valid ID card of college to register. Then accordingly tests will be taken to see which company did you get!

Will I recieve a certificate of participation?

Yes.

What other events other than placement events will be conducted?

Apart from placements, we will conduct entertainment events, star night, bollywood dance competition. We are planning to have a big social event as well.

How do you select team members for the placement fest?

Good Question! Any one who is dedicated and wants to help develope DTU is welcome to the team. See contact us and drop a mail to us! Cheers

Are there passes needed for the event?

Yes! The passes will be provided at various places before the fest! Evrything is carefully thought of!

Can I register online?

Yes.

What all companies will come at the fest?

See the fb page(see contact us) for the list of companies!

Are all the events will be paid?

No, Some of them will be paid, to encourage serious participations, although entertainment events are likely to be free!
